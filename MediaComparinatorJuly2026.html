All Formulation Sources

    Calculation method: only salt masses (mg/L) are stored per formulation. Ion concentrations (both molar and mass) and salt molarities are all derived at load time from those masses using fixed molecular weights and reaction stoichiometry — they are never entered twice. This removes the class of bug where an "ion view" and a "salt view" of the same medium quietly disagree with each other. Formulations flagged ⚠ unverified have salt masses that are internally self-consistent (the math checks out) but have not been confirmed against a primary published source — verify before using in production.
